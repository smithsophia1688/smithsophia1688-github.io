About

Sophia is a graduate student at the University of Texas at Austin within the Oden Institute for Computational Science and Engineering. She researches autonomous systems with the Center for Autonomy, focusing on multi-agent interactions and learning. The past two summers, Sophia interned at NASA Ames Research Center working on projects related to contingency management for unmanned aircrafts.

For her undergraduate studies, Sophia completed her bachelors degree in Mathematics and Physics at the University of Chicago.

In her free time, Sophia likes to run, nordic ski, read, and make art.
