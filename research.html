Decentralized Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

My research in this area focuses on developing algorithms that enable autonomuos agents to learn and make decisions collaboratively in complex environments.

Decentralized Conflict Resolution for Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning Through Shared Scheduling Protocols

Leveraging shared scheduling protocols to resolve conflict during decentralized multi-agent reinforcement learning. Shared scheduling protocols decentrally coordinate agent behavior and ensure no one agent is allowed to greedily use resources. See GitHub repository.

Automatic Decomposition of Reward Machines for Decentralized Multiagent Reinforcement Learning

Investigating methods for automatically decomposing complex reward structures into agent specific components to enable fully decentralized learning.

Game Theory

My research explores the application of game-theoretic concepts to inferable behavior and learning.

Encouraging Inferable Behavior for Autonomy: Repeated Bimatrix Stackelberg Games with Observations

Investigating the use of repeated bimatrix Stackelberg games to encourage inferable behavior in autonomous agents when they are observed by other agents.

Function Encoders for Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

Designing multi-agent reinforcement learning algorithms to leverage learned representation of opponents and game-theoretic reasoning.

Bridging AI and Formal Methods

This research investigates how formal method can be used alongside AI techniques to improve the design of multi-agent systems.

Large Language Models for Verifiable Multi-Agent Team Decomposition

Iteratively using language models to generate and refine formal specifications for multi-agent systems, enabling automated verification and validation of compositional multi-agent behavior.

Air Traffic Management for Autonomous Aircraft

Natural Language Processing of Non-Towered Airport Radio Communications

Investigating the integration of unmanned aircraft into non-towered airports by leveraging radio communications for situational awareness and planning around non-towered airports via natural language processing.

Communication during Loss Command and Control Link Events at Non-Towered Airports

Concept development evaluating communication capabilities and requirements for unmanned aircraft when flying into non-towered airports. Investigating possible contingency operation adjustments in the event a remote pilot loses contact with an unmanned aircraft.
